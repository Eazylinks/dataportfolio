  2. The results show which products and categories are driving the most revenue across different years. High average sales in specific categories indicate strong customer demand, suggesting these categories should be a focus for stock replenishment and marketing. Conversely, categories with lower averages may need promotional efforts or further market analysis.

Higher GDP levels generally correspond to increased sales, suggesting that the company’s performance is positively correlated with economic growth. This insight can be used for long-term strategic planning and adjusting business operations based on economic forecasts.

  10. The analysis shows how promotions influence average sales quantities across different product categories and products. Categories and products with significant sales increases during promotions should continue to be targeted in marketing campaigns, as they show high responsiveness to promotional activities.
  11. The insight indicates which product categories performed best during the crucial holiday season in December 2022. Categories with strong sales during this period should be the focus of future holiday promotions, while those that underperformed may require different marketing approaches or deeper discounts.
